## Introduction
This course aims to explore the vast expanse of world history from prehistoric times to the contemporary age of 2026, using the books "Sapiens" by Yuval Noah Harari, "Unstoppable Us" by Yuval Noah Harari, and "Guns, Germs, and Steel" by Jared Diamond as foundational texts. Each lesson will provide learners with insights into historical trends, sociopolitical dynamics, technological advancements, and cultural developments that have shaped human civilization.
